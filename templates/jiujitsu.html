Blog 06182019

Last Sunday a few of our team members participated in a local tournament. While some teammates did well, others, unfortunately, did not. It is a part of the game that everyone goes through. One of our coaches had this to say:

"Accept your wins graciosly and study your loss ferociously.
In that loss is the secret to your future wins."

That had a profound impact for me. No, I didn't compete this weekend, but that advice resonated and is applicable with anything in life.
